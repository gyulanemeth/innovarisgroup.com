About Innovaris Group

Innovaris Group LLC is an email infrastructure and systems company. We build products and services for businesses that take email seriously — from authentication and deliverability to automation and operational scalability.

Founder

Photo placeholder

Gyula Németh

Founder & CEO

Gyula has been working with HTML email systems since 2013. Over more than a decade, he has helped build multiple enterprise and SaaS products focused on email infrastructure, rendering technology, automation systems, and operational workflows.

His work spans the full email technology stack — from deliverability infrastructure and rendering engines to automation architecture and multi-client operational systems — giving him an unusually complete view of what makes email communication systems work at scale.

He is the founder of Innovaris Group LLC and the creator of BlueFox Email.

Background

Our experience spans the full lifecycle of email system development — from early embeddable editors to modern SaaS infrastructure.

  • EDMdesigner — one of the first embeddable drag-and-drop email editor plugins
  • Chamaileon — a collaborative email design platform and SDK
  • EmailHero — an email automation and campaign platform
  • BlueFox Email — a scalable email automation and communication platform for SaaS and agencies
  • Large-scale multi-brand communication systems for publishers and media organizations
  • Deliverability, rendering, and operational email architecture across complex environments
